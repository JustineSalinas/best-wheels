# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Filipino car buyers in Iloilo City and nearby provinces looking for affordable, verified 2nd-hand and bank-repossessed vehicles. They are value-conscious, may be first-time car buyers or upgraders, and often depend on Facebook for discovery — but need a trustworthy, professional presence to feel confident making a large purchase decision.

## Product Purpose

Best Wheels Car Display is Iloilo City's trusted local dealer for bank repos, slightly-used, and 2nd-hand cars. The website exists to give the dealership a permanent, SEO-visible home that works 24/7 — solving the core problem that their entire presence is locked behind a Facebook login. The site must showcase the full inventory in an organized, filterable way; make inquiries frictionless; and build trust with buyers before they walk through the door.

## Brand Personality

Friendly · Transparent · Local

The brand feels like the trusted neighbor who knows cars — not a corporate chain, not a fly-by-night classifieds listing. Warm, confident, proud to be Iloilo. Copy is direct and helpful, never salesy or jargon-heavy.

## Anti-references

- OLX / Carousell: cluttered listing aesthetic, spam-font, zero visual identity, no brand trust
- Plain blog / whitepage: walls of text, no visual hierarchy, looks unfinished
- Generic corporate dealer site: stock-photo heavy, bland blue-gray palette, lifeless — could be any brand anywhere

## Design Principles

1. **Local pride, not generic dealer.** Every design choice should feel like it belongs to a specific Iloilo business, not a template.
2. **Trust before transaction.** Show the evidence first — verified units, updated docs, lifetime check-up, washes included. The buyer needs to trust the dealer before they inquire.
3. **Organized transparency.** The inventory is browsable, filterable, and clear. No hiding prices behind "message for price" friction — the form is the inquiry.
4. **Red speaks, white delivers.** The brand red carries confidence and energy. White sections carry the content clearly. Never muddy the two.
5. **Mobile is the primary device.** Iloilo buyers browse on phones. Every element is touch-friendly and loads fast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body text, 3:1 large text)
- Semantic HTML throughout
- All interactive elements keyboard-navigable
- Form labels explicit, not placeholder-only
- Reduced motion respected
